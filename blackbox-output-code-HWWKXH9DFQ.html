Our Story

A Taste of Tradition in France

Welcome to The Golden Spice. Located in the beautiful town of Oyonnax, we bring the heart of India to your table. We believe that food is not just about eating, but about experiencing culture.

Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest local ingredients available in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region. Whether it's our slow-cooked Tandoori meats or our freshly baked Naan, every dish tells a story of heritage and passion.
